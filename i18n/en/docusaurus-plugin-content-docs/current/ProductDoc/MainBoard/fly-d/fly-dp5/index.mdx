# Introduction

> Mellow Fly-DP5 is a high-performance 3D printer motherboard developed by the Fly3D mainboard R&D team of Guangzhou Melun Electronic Technology Co., Ltd., supporting up to 5 stepper motors.

<ImageView image={require('@site/docs/ProductDoc/MainBoard/fly-d/fly-dp5/img/dp5.png').default} size="60%" align="center" />

## **1. Product Features**

* Uses a 32-bit ARM Cortex-M0+ series STM32F072RBT6 main control chip with a frequency of 48MHz
* Supports CAN bus connection, providing more stable data transmission, lower latency, and more stable connections
* Driver support: DP2209 and DP560
* Board-mounted DIAG function pins, allowing for easy use of unlimited homing support through simple jumper cap insertion/removal

## 2. Product Specifications

* MCU: STM32F072RBT6
* Firmware: Klipper/Marlin
* Supports 12-24V DC power supply, providing more stable power supply and eliminating the need for a voltage reduction module
* Driver operation modes supported: SPI, UART, STEP/DIR
* Supported drivers: External TMC5160Pro, TMC5160, TMC2209, TMC2225, TMC2226, TMC2208, TMC2130, A4988, etc.
* Expansion interfaces: BLTouch (Servos, Probe), UART X1, 12864 X1
* Fans: Two controllable fans X2, two parallel fans X2, two always-on fans X2
* Temperature sensors: Two NTC 100K channels
* Heating interfaces: Two controllable interfaces supporting 10A
* Supported machine structures: Cartesian, Delta, Kossel, Ultimaker, CoreXY
