## Extrude Page

    <ImageView image={require('@site/docs/ProductDoc/ExtensionBoard/fly-halo/img/jichu-1.png').default} size="50%" align="left" />
    <ImageView image={require('@site/docs/ProductDoc/ExtensionBoard/fly-halo/img/jichu-2.png').default} size="50%" align="left" />
    * The operable areas in Image 1 above correspond to the numbers below
    1. Click: Increase extruder target temperature by 1
    2. Long press: Enter extrusion temperature setting, adjust temperature by dragging the ring, click again to confirm the set temperature
    3. Click: Decrease extruder target temperature by 1
    4. Click: Extrude
    5. Up and down swipe: Select the length of extrusion or retraction, unit is mm
    6. Click: Retract
    7. Long press: Enter extrusion speed setting in Image 2, select extrusion speed by swiping up and down, unit is mm/s, click the surrounding blank area after selection to exit
