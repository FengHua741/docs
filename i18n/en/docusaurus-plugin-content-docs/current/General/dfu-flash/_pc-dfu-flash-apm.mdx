    1. Flashing Tool <Button variant="contained" disableElevation href="https://mellow-next.klipper.cn/docs/ResDownload/auxiliary_software/">Download Link</Button>
    2. Download `DFUProgrammer`
    3. Copy the previously compiled firmware (klipper.bin) or the pre-compiled `Katapult firmware` to any directory on your computer
    4. Connect the motherboard to the computer using a Type-C data cable, and ensure it has entered **DFU flashing mode**
    5. Open DFUProgrammer and follow the instructions below
        <ImageView image={require('@site/docs/General/dfu-flash/img/apmdfu.png').default} size="100%" align="center" />
        <ImageView image={require('@site/docs/General/dfu-flash/img/apmdfu1.png').default} size="100%" align="center" />
    6. Click to start updating the firmware and wait for it to complete. After the flashing is done, you can remove the Type-C cable and jumpers.
